About Us🫶🏼


Nestled amidst the rolling hills of Valencia, Spain, the Orange Soda Company emerged from a simple yet extraordinary vision: to bottle the essence of sunshine and the zest of life. Our tale begins with Maria Ramirez👱🏼‍♀️, a spirited explorer of flavors, who found herself captivated by the citrus symphony of the region's lush Orange orchards🍊.
In the heart💓 of these sun-soaked groves, Maria's passion ignited. Guided by an unwavering commitment to authenticity, she embarked on a journey to craft the quintessential orange soda.
Collaborating with local farmers👨🏼‍🌾 who shared her reverence for nature, Maria sourced the juiciest, hand-picked oranges to form the core of her creation.
Years of meticulous experimentation led to the birth of a remarkable concoction - a harmonious blend of Valencia's finest oranges.
With each batch, Maria perfected the balance between natural sweetness and the invigorating tang that characterizes the perfected Orange Soda🥤.
Today, Orange Soda Company's effervescent libations grace tables worldwide. In every sip, the legacy of Maria's devotion unfolds, inviting you to relish the culmination of a citrus-infused dream. The symphony of bubbles and the citrus crescendo invite you to savor life's vibrant moments.
At Orange Soda Company, we don't merely create a beverage; we encapsulate the essence of a sunlit morning, the joy of discovery, and the celebration of simple pleasures. Join us in raising a glass to the spirit of adventure, the heritage of taste, and the legacy of Maria's citrus-inspired journey. Cheers to a world where each bottle holds not just a drink, but an embodiment of the extraordinary.
Welcome to the Orange Soda Company experience.
